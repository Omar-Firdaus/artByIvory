Handmade in Montana. Custom-painted Kinco mittens for ski country—built to last, made to stand out.

From the mountain up

01

Handmade in ski country

Ivory started painting Kincos because they’re the mittens you see on every lift op, every patroller, every local at the hill. Durable, warm, built for work. We take that blank canvas and make each pair one of a kind.

02

Built to last

Kinco leather holds paint. We hand-paint each design and bake it in—no peeling, no cracking. These gloves aren’t shelf decor. They’re made for cold mornings, deep days, and seasons of use.

03

One of a kind

No two pairs are alike. When you wear ART BY IVORY, you’re wearing something that exists nowhere else. Custom mitten commissions? We do those too.
